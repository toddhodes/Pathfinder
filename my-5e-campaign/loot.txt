
Rod of Uk'atoa
--------------
(based on Quicksilver + base rod + depth behavior)

Vestige of the Divergence
Wondrous Item, legendary (requires attunement by aquatic race)

Sentience: (Partial; Neutral)
The weapon communicates telepathically at 120' range,
and speaks common, undercommon, and all aquatic languages

DORMANT
- Acts as a rod of retribution
- At depth of 30' or lower, +1 AC and +1 saving throws

AWAKENED
- At all times, +1 AC and +1 saving throws
- At depth of 10' or lower, +2 AC and +2 saving throws
- Is a melee weapon you have proficiency & the finesse property, +2 to attack and damage, 1d6 dmg
- Retribution damage becomes 3d10
- The saving throw DC for the weapon’s abilities increases to 15

EXALTED
- At all times, +2 AC and +2 saving throws
- When touching water, +3 AC and +3 saving throws
- The weapon’s bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +3.
- When thrown, it returns to your hand.
- The saving throw DC for the weapon’s abilities increases to 17.
- Retribution damage becomes 4d10


Moonweaver's Spear
------------------
Vestige of the Divergence
Weapon (spear), legendary (requires attunement)

Sentience: (Partial; Neutral)
The weapon communicates telepathically at 120' range,
and speaks common, halfling, elven, celestial, and sylvan

DORMANT
- Moonweaver's Touch. 3/day, action, expend 1 or more charges to shoot a beam of moonlight at a creature you can see within 30 feet. 
   The target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 2d10 radiant damage for the first charge, +1d10 for each additional
   charge expended this way on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. On a failed save, the target is also transformed into
   a tiny beast with a challenge rating of 0 (your choice) for 1 minute, as though affected by a polymorph spell.
   The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

AWAKENED
- +2 hit & dmg
- returns to thrower after a ranged attack
- sentience access: 1/day, can ask it a Y/N question.  10% chance of divine insight answer 

EXALTED
- +3 hit & dmg
- saving throw DC increases to 17.


Hide of the Feral Guardian
--------------------------
Vestige of the Divergence
Armor (studded leather), legendary (requires attunement)

Dormant State
- The armor grants you a +1 bonus to AC.
- While you are transformed by an effect that replaces any of your game statistics with those of another creature, you have a +1 bonus to melee attack and damage rolls, and you retain the benefits of this armor.
- As an action, you can use the armor to cast polymorph on yourself, transforming into a giant owl while retaining your Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores. This property can’t be used again until the next dawn.

Awakened State
- The AC bonus of the armor increases to +2.
- While you are transformed by an effect that replaces any of your game statistics with those of another creature, your bonus to melee attack and damage rolls increases by 1 (to +2).
- When you cast the polymorph spell using this armor, you can transform into a cave bear (use the polar bear statistics).

Exalted State
- The AC bonus of the armor increases to +3.
- While you are transformed by an effect that replaces any of your game statistics with those of another creature, your bonus to melee attack and damage rolls increases by 1 (to +3).
- When you cast the polymorph spell using this armor, you can transform into a guardian wolf.

Lute of the Beacon
------------------
(based on Lute of Inspiration)

Vestige of the Divergence
Wondrous Item, legendary (requires attunement by a by a devotee of the Luxon)

A dark wood lute with gunmetal silver etchings, dragonbone inlays, and strings of adamatinum alloy.
various symbols of the gods of music is carved into the back. (Corellon Larethian, Olidammara)

DORMANT
- spellcasting focus for your bard spells.
- +1 spell attack rolls and save DC.
- +1/day Bardic Inspiration.

AWAKENED
- +2 spell attack rolls and save DC.
- +2/day Bardic Inspiration.
- +d4 bonus to initiative rolls.
- 1/week: Commune with the Beacon (as per the spell)
- Song of Rest - creatures roll an extra die when regaining hit points.  If no dice are used, gain 1 die.

EXALTED
- +3 spell attack rolls and save DC.
- +3/day Bardic Inspiration.
- +d6 bonus to initiative rolls.
- jack of all trades uses full proficiency bonus
- Learn any spell of your choosing; does not count against the number of spells you can know.

Other possible properties
-------------------------
- choose a skill or tool proficiency -- your proficiency bonus is double for ability checks made using these skills or tools
- as a free action at the start of each of your turns, you can change the weapon type of the weapon.
- While wielding this weapon, you can cast the disguise self spell (DC 15) at will, without expending a spell slot.


The Verdant Heart
-------------------
Wondrous Item, legendary (requires attunement by a Druid)

The Verdant Heart is a green pendant on a necklace. You can use the pendant as a spellcasting
focus for your spells while wearing or holding it, and you gain 1 additional use your Wildshape
feature once per long rest.



Sapling
--------
Wondrous Item, legendary (requires attunement)

Sapling is a staff made from a branch of the Seed of Rebirth in Vasselheim. It acts as a +1
quarterstaff, and adds +1 to spell attack rolls and spell DC while attuned to it.
