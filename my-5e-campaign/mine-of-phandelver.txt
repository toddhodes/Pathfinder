# Phandalin

## Stonehill Inn 

Rumors:
- Narth, an old farmer: "Sister Garaele, who oversees the Shrine of Luck, recently left
town for a few days, then returned wounded and exhausted." "Shrine of Luck" section

- Elsa, a gossipy barmaid: "Daran Edermath, the orchard keeper, is a former adventurer."
"Edermath Orchard" section

- Lanar, a miner: "Orc raiders have been seen on the east end of Triboar Trail. The
townmaster is looking for someone to run them off." "Townmaster's Hall" section

- Trilena, the innkeeper's wife: "Thel Dendrar, a local woodcarver, stood up to the
Redbrands a tenday ago when they came by his shop and leered at his wife. The ruffians
murdered him. Several townsfolk saw it happen. The Redbrands grabbed his body, and now
his wife, daughter, and son have gone missing too." (Redbrands took Thel's wife and
children to their secret hideout.)

- Pip, Toblen's young son: "Qelline Alderleaf's son Carp said he found a secret tunnel in
the woods, but Redbrands almost caught him." "Alderleaf Farm" section

- Freda, a weaver: "The Redbrands hassle every business in town, except for the Phandalin
Miner's Exchange. They don't want trouble with Halia Thornton, who runs it." "Phandalin
Miner's Exchange" section

## "Shrine of Luck" 
- banshee Agatha 
  bring jeweled silver comb, tell location of spellbook belonging to a legendary mage named Bowgentle. 
  3 potions of healing "Conyberry and Agatha's Lair"

## "Phandalin Miner's Exchange" 
- Halia Offer: Redbrand ruffians loiter around the Sleeping Giant tap house and
  have a base under Tresendar Manor.  100gp to eliminate Redbrand leader, Glasstaff, and bring correspondence.  
  DC 15 Wisdom (Insight) ulterior motives

## "Edermath Orchard" 
- knows the Redbrands hang around the Sleeping Giant tap house, but he can also tell the
characters that the main Redbrand safe house lies under Tresendar Manor, the ruin at the
east edge of town.

- prospectors in the hills northeast of Phandalin digging around in the
ruins known as Old Owl Well.  chased from the area by undead. 
knows that the ruins are an old watchtower of an ancient magical empire

## "Townmaster's Hall" / returning Sildar Hallwinter 
Quest: Orc Trouble: head east on Triboar Trail, band of orcs near Wyvern Tor. 100 gp 
Quest: Find Cragmaw Castle: find the lost mine of Wave Echo Cave
       500gp locate and defeat chieftain.  search around Triboar Trail / "Wilderness Encounters" 
Quest: Finding Iarno / 200gp eliminate redbrands (Glassstaff)
  Iarno Albrek, Lords' Alliance, disappeared exploring Tresendar Manor two months ago, shortly after arriving 
  investigate the manor to find Iarno "a short, dark-bearded human wizard in his thirties."

## Quests Summary

 - Sister Garaele seek banshee Agatha in the ruined town of Conybery and ask about Bowgentle's spellbook.
 - Daran Edermath wants to find out who is lurking near the ruins at Old Owl Well.
 - Quelline Alderleaf go to the ruined town of Thundertree and consult with the druid Reidoth, 
   whereabouts of Cragmaw Castle, Wave Echo Cave, or both.
 - Townmaster Harbin Wester seek out orc encampment near Wyvern Tor and chase away 
 - Sildar Hallwinter wants the characters to find Cragmaw Castle, rescue Gundren Rockseeker and map.
